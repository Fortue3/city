Water Conservation Initiative 2025

The City of Gweru is launching a Water Conservation Initiative starting March 20, 2025, aimed at promoting sustainable water use across the city. This initiative addresses the growing need to manage water resources efficiently in the Midlands Province.

With the theme "Every Drop Counts", we’re encouraging residents, businesses, and institutions to adopt water-saving practices to ensure a reliable supply for future generations.

Key Measures

  • Installation of water-efficient fixtures in public buildings by June 2025.
  • Subsidized rainwater harvesting kits for households, available from April 1, 2025.
  • Public awareness campaigns with workshops starting March 25, 2025, at the Gweru Civic Centre.
  • Stricter regulations on water usage for commercial properties, effective May 1, 2025.
  • Partnerships with local schools to educate students on water conservation.

How to Participate

  • Workshops: Attend free sessions at the Gweru Civic Centre every Tuesday and Thursday from March 25 to April 30, 2025, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM.
  • Rainwater Kits: Apply at City Hall or online at services.gweru.org.zw starting April 1, 2025.
  • Reports: Report leaks or wastage via the "Report a Fault" page on our website.
