Gweru Agricultural Show 2025

The 99th Gweru Agricultural Show will take place from April 15-18, 2025, at the Gweru Showgrounds, located in Gweru, Zimbabwe. This annual event celebrates the region’s rich agricultural heritage and vibrant local culture.

This year’s theme, "Sustainable Farming for a Thriving Future", emphasizes eco-friendly farming practices and economic development.

What to Expect

  • Over 150 exhibitors showcasing innovative agricultural technologies, livestock displays, and farm produce.
  • Educational workshops on sustainable farming techniques.
  • Live performances by top Zimbabwean artists.
  • A first-ever drone technology demonstration.
  • Entertainment including car burnouts and cultural displays.

Event Details

  • Dates: April 15-18, 2025
  • Hours: Gates open daily at 8:00 AM and close at 6:00 PM
  • Location: Gweru Showgrounds, Gweru, Zimbabwe
  • Tickets: $5 for adults, $2 for children under 12
  • Parking: Free parking available on-site
